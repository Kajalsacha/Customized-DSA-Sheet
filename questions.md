### 💡 Interviewer's Note
* **Stacks:** Usually used for depth-first processing or backtracking.
* **Queues:** Essential for level-order processing (BFS) and maintaining order in time-series data.
* **Complexity:** Aim for $O(N)$ time complexity for most Stack/Queue problems by avoiding nested loops.
